Online Activities

Finding activities/lessons to encourage online safety is a fun, interactive way to teach your child. These activities can help your child learn good habits and safe ways to navigate the online world. Here are a few ideas for activities you can do with your children:

  • Play video games with your children! Not only is this great for bonding, but it also creates a safe space for communication

  • Navigate through your childâ€™s favorite sites/apps and teach them how to browse safely

  • Create a safety contract together with your child and address all their concerns/worries

  • Find fun videos online that you can watch with your children

  • Find another family you can trust, and set up an E-Pen Pal to encourage positive communication between children
