About Us

We are a team of Publicis Sapient interns who are dedicated to assisting working parents whose children now stay at home due to the ongoing pandemic.

We want to assist these parents in any way we can. With this website, we aim to provide resources that help parents entertain their children in a productive and easy manner. Through our social media outreach, we hope to help as many families as possible.

One way you can help is by spreading the word about us and sharing our resources with others. If you have any suggestions or recommendations for content, feel free to contact us!
