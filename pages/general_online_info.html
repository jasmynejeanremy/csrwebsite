General Information

Keeping your children safe online is an important task that requires diligence. Communicating openly about the risks of the internet and how to avoid them can help your child stay safe online. As a parent, it is important to stay educated about different online platforms, the risks involved, and measures to create a safe environment. Here are a few general tips to help ensure your childâ€™s online safety:

  • Create family rules for sharing content, screen time, etc.

  • Explain to your child how to avoid suspicious messages, links, or emails

  • Encourage positive communication online

  • Bookmark favorite sites

  • Monitor any computer, tablet, phone, etc. time and check for any unfamiliar account charges

  • Look for warning signs of your child being targeted by an online predator

  • Ask your child not to share any personal information online

  • Search up parental controls and security settings according to your device, browser, etc.
