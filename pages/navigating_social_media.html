Navigating Social Media

As children become more tech-savvy and connected, it is increasingly vital that parents maintain a strong sense of security when it comes to children’s online social activity. If your child wants to or is already using social media, check out some of these tips to help keep them safe:

  • Regularly check privacy settings on all social media platforms

  • Research the different social media platforms if you are not familiar with them all

  • Create guidelines/rules for posting, sharing content, and chatting with others

  • Make sure your child’s profile is private and advise them not to accept follower/friend requests from strangers

  • Let your child know you are always there for them, and encourage positive communication online

  • Remind your child to think twice before posting something and that their actions have consequences

  • Make sure your child knows how to report or block things
