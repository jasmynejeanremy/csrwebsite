Fun Activities

  • Create a Comic Strip

    Making a comic strip is creative and fun! Think of some characters and situations to put them in. You can use this website for some advice and help if you need that to get start: http://www.makebeliefscomix.com/Comix/

  • Write a script for your favorite TV show or make one up yourself

    Sometimes, creating your own episode of your favorite TV show instead of watching it can be a new refreshing experience. Let your imagination run wild and think of the endless possibilities you can create.

  • Write a handwritten letter to your person of choice

    Take a step back and try crafting your own handwritten letter to any person you want. Handwriting letters helps you focus your thoughts and the visual of your handwriting gives your letter even more personality and thought.

  • Try making something such as building a robot

    f you're into technology, you can try to build your own robot! The tutorial below will teach you how to make a simple beetle robot using easily obtainable parts

  • Start a small business

    Finding something you’re passionate about and making money from it is a great thing! Get ahead and create your own business to learn the ins and outs of entrepreneurship.

    • Crafts for Cash: Take your passion for making crafts and earn some quick cash as well by selling them to friends, family,or even strangers!
    • Yard Care Services: If you like being outside then you should try a yard care business! Potential services can include cleaning yards up, mowing lawns, maintaining gardens, planting vegetables and much more
    • If you love technology you can turn your knowledge of computers and devices into a business. Help people troubleshoot their computer problems and remove pesky viruses that people may have.

  • Go on a scavenger hunt

    Create a list of items for your friends or siblings to find inside or outdoors.

  • Put together a time capsule

    Collect items that represent the time you are living in. You can also leave money inside the time capsule as a gift to your future self. After, write a letter to your future self and leave it inside the capsule as well. Once it’s all set, put it in a hiding spot for you to find and open up at a future time.

  • Learn a coding language

    If you love computers and cannot find a way to take your eyes offthe screen, you may want to cultivate that interest and learn a coding language. Rather than consuming digital content, you will be creating it, which is much more rewarding. There are plenty of great free resources available below.

    • https://scratch.mit.edu/
    • https://www.codecademy.com/
    • https://www.khanacademy.org/
